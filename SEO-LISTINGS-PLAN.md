## Auto vs Manual — summary for programmatic action

**Effectively ALL of these are MANUAL** — every reputable directory requires an owner account plus
phone/email OTP or credential verification, specifically to prevent automated/spam listings. There is
**no free programmatic (API) bulk-submit path** for these Pakistani + global directories. The realistic
"automation" is:
- **Bing Places**: one-click **import from Google Business Profile** (near-auto).
- **Facebook/Instagram**: can be created via Meta Business Suite but still need manual verification.
- Everything else: manual form + OTP.

Recommended sequence: **1 (GBP polish) → 2 (Bing import) → 4,5,6,7 (PK health) → 8,9 (social, for `sameAs`) → 13–20 (PK dirs) → 21 (niche).**
Do Facebook + Instagram early so their URLs can be added as `sameAs` in the homepage schema (below).
