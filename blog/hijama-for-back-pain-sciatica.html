Why your back & leg hurtآپ کی کمر اور ٹانگ کیوں دکھتی ہے

Long hours at a desk or behind the wheel through Karachi traffic, lifting heavy loads, standing all day, sleeping on a sagging mattress, carrying extra weight — everyday life is hard on the lower back. Muscles along the spine tighten, knot and stay tense, and that dull, nagging ache can settle in for weeks. When a tight, inflamed muscle presses on the nerve that runs from your lower back through your hip and down your leg, you get sciatica: that unmistakable sharp, shooting or burning line of pain down one leg, sometimes with tingling or a heavy, numb feeling. It can make sitting, standing and even sleeping miserable. ڈیسک پر یا کراچی کی ٹریفک میں گاڑی چلاتے لمبے گھنٹے، بھاری وزن اٹھانا، سارا دن کھڑے رہنا، ڈھلکے ہوئے گدے پر سونا، اضافی وزن اٹھانا — روزمرہ زندگی کمر پر بھاری پڑتی ہے۔ ریڑھ کی ہڈی کے ساتھ کے پٹھے سخت ہو جاتے، گرہ بن جاتے اور تناؤ میں رہتے ہیں، اور وہ مدھم، مسلسل درد ہفتوں تک بیٹھ جاتا ہے۔ جب کوئی اکڑا، سوجا ہوا پٹھا اس عصب پر دباؤ ڈالتا ہے جو آپ کی نچلی کمر سے کولہے اور ٹانگ تک جاتا ہے، تو عرق النسا ہوتا ہے: وہ واضح، تیز، اترتا یا جلتا ہوا درد جو ایک ٹانگ کے نیچے جاتا ہے، کبھی جھنجھناہٹ یا بھاری، سُن ہونے کے احساس کے ساتھ۔ یہ بیٹھنا، کھڑا ہونا حتیٰ کہ سونا بھی دشوار بنا دیتا ہے۔

How Hijama helps back painحجامہ کمر درد میں کیسے مدد دیتا ہے

Hijama (wet cupping) is a trusted traditional approach to exactly this kind of muscular back and neck pain. The cups draw up the skin and the tight muscle beneath, easing tension and inviting fresh blood flow to a stiff, tired area — and many patients tell us the relief begins the moment the cups come off. It is the same Sunnah practice the Prophet ﷺ himself was treated with, done here with modern, single-use sterile equipment. That shooting pain down the legٹانگ میں اترتا ہوا درد

Sciatica has a way of taking over your day — you can't find a comfortable way to sit, the drive to work is agony, and the pain wakes you at night. Because sciatic pain travels along a nerve, we don't just work on the spot that hurts; we follow the whole line, placing cups across the lower back and along the hip, glute and upper leg where the pain runs. Many patients feel that sharp, electric line soften into something far more bearable, and tell us they can finally sit, drive and sleep again. What the research suggestsتحقیق کیا بتاتی ہے

Back and neck pain is the area where cupping has the strongest support in modern research: several studies suggest cupping can ease chronic back and neck pain, with people reporting less pain and easier movement after a course of sessions. That fits exactly what we see every day in the clinic — patients who arrived stiff and hurting, leaving looser, lighter and more comfortable. What a session is likeسیشن کیسا ہوتا ہے

Your first visit begins with us listening — where the pain sits, where it travels, what makes it worse. Then you lie down comfortably, we apply gentle warmth, and place the cups. There is a light, drawing pressure, then a few tiny, near-painless scratches, and the cups do their work for a short while. It is calmer and gentler than most people expect. Afterwards you'll have a few circular marks that fade over several days — a normal, harmless part of Hijama, not a bruise from injury.
